AC Milan is one of the most prestigious and successful football clubs in the world, with a rich history that dates back to its founding in 1899. Based in Milan, Italy, the club has won 19 Serie A titles and 7 UEFA Champions League trophies, making it one of the most decorated teams in European football.

Known for its passionate fan base, iconic red and black colors, and a legacy of legendary players such as Paolo Maldini, Marco van Basten, and Alessandro Nesta, AC Milan continues to be a symbol of excellence and tradition in the sport.
